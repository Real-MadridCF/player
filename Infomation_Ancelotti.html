Ancelotti

Bio

Carlo Ancelotti, born on June 10, 1959, is an Italian manager for Real Madrid. He has led top clubs including AC Milan, Chelsea, and Bayern Munich. Ancelotti first joined Real Madrid in 2013 and won the Champions League in 2014, then returned in 2021. Known for his tactical expertise and calm demeanor, he has been crucial to the team's success.
